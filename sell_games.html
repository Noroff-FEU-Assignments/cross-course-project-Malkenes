Sell

1

List your games

Log in to your Gamehub account

Find the game you want to sell

Choose the condition of your game

Upload clear images of the game and its packaging

Set your preferred shipping options and policies (e.g., shipping cost, return policy).

2

Manage your listings

Regularly check your seller dashboard for messages and notifications.

Keep your game listings up to date. If a game is sold, mark it as "sold" or remove it.

Adjust your prices and listings based on market demand and competition.

3

Complete Sales and Ship Games

When a buyer makes a purchase, you'll receive a notification.

Carefully package the game to ensure it arrives in the condition described.

Ship the game to the buyer using the selected shipping method.

Update the order status and provide tracking information if available.

Be transparent about the estimated delivery time.

4

Receive payment

Gamehub typically releases funds to sellers after successful delivery and a satisfied buyer.

Payment methods may include direct deposits, PayPal, or other payment gateways.
